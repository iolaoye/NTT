Soils Page

The Soils page allows the user to add Layers, edit, and delete the soils for the particular field selected in the Field Page and that he or she would like to use for the simulation. Depending on the Location method that was selected, the user has to enter varying amounts of information. The information below is organized by the Location method that was selected. Regardless of the method that was selected however there are 4 general rules that apply to all methods. They are as follows.

1. Each of the soils has to have AT LEAST one layer. You can view whether a soil has layers by clicking on the icon in the actions column. This will show the layers information for this particular soil in Layers information. If the Soil does not have layers, the user needs to manually enter them.

2. If the total soil percentage of the selected soils is less than 100%, NTT will adjust the values accordingly. The soil percentage is seen on the right hand corner of the soils characteristics. To edit the percentages of the soils, click on the icon in the Action column.

3. However, the total soil percentage of the selected soils cannot be more than 100% or equal to 0%. If this is the case, NTT will automatically adjust the values.

4. User can delete soil(s) by clicking icon under Action column.

Click Save And Continue to go to the scenarios page.
